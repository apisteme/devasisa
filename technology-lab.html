Technology Lab

Empowering young minds with digital literacy and programming skills

About Our Technology Lab

The Technology Lab at Devaśīṣa Gurukulam is designed to prepare students for the digital future. We combine fundamental programming concepts with hands-on projects in robotics and digital literacy, creating an engaging and practical learning environment.

Programming Basics

Learn fundamental programming concepts through interactive coding exercises and projects.

Robotics Workshop

Build and program robots while learning about mechanics, electronics, and automation.

Digital Literacy

Master essential digital tools and develop responsible technology usage habits.

Project-Based Learning

Apply technology skills to real-world projects and creative challenges.

Program Details

  • Weekly hands-on sessions of 90 minutes
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • Age-appropriate programming languages and tools
  • Access to state-of-the-art equipment
  • Take-home projects and coding exercises
  • Regular tech showcases and competitions

Learning Outcomes

Students who participate in our Technology Lab will:

  • Develop strong programming fundamentals
  • Gain hands-on experience with robotics
  • Master essential digital tools and applications
  • Learn computational thinking and problem-solving
  • Build confidence in technology creation
  • Understand responsible technology usage
