Math Circle

Discover the beauty and power of mathematics through interactive learning

About Our Math Circle

The Math Circle at Devaśīṣa Gurukulam is designed to nurture mathematical thinking and problem-solving abilities in students. We combine traditional Vedic mathematical techniques with modern pedagogical approaches to create an engaging and enriching learning experience.

Vedic Mathematics

Learn ancient Indian mathematical techniques that enhance mental calculation abilities and mathematical intuition.

Problem Solving

Develop critical thinking skills through challenging problems and mathematical puzzles.

Interactive Learning

Engage in hands-on activities and group discussions that make mathematics fun and accessible.

Maths Olympiad

Challenge your child to participate in Maths Olympiad and open a world of opportunities in the field of Mathematics

Program Details

  • Run by students and teachers of Mathematics
  • Access to world class resources and practice materials
  • Weekly sessions of 90 minutes each
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • Age-appropriate curriculum for different grade levels
  • Regular assessments and progress tracking

Learning Outcomes

Students who participate in our Math Circle will:

  • Develop strong foundational mathematical skills
  • Improve logical reasoning and analytical abilities
  • Practice mathematical proof techniques
  • Gain confidence in problem-solving
  • Appreciate the cultural heritage of Mathematics
